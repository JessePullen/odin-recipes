Smokey Bacon Pizza Recipe

Description

This smokey bacon pizza is ready in just 20 minutes, faster than you can order take-away.

Ingredients

  • 2 x 20cm pre-made pizza bases
  • ⅓ cup pizza sauce or passata
  • ½ small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 ½ cups grated mozzarella or pizza cheese
  • 200g pkt DON Crafted Cuts Double Smoked Bacon
  • Barbecue sauce, for serving
  • Oregano, for serving

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 220°C (200°C fan-forced).
  2. Place pizza bases on pizza or oven trays.
    Spread each base evenly with the pizza sauce, scatter over the onion and the cheese.
  3. Place 3 rashers of bacon on each pizza, scrunch up a little so they are not lying flat and drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  4. Place into the oven and cook for approx. 15 minutes, or until bacon and base are crispy.
    Drizzle a little barbecue sauce over pizza and scatter with oregano leaves. Enjoy.
